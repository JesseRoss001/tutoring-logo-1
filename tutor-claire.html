About Claire

Mathematics, English, and Biology tutor for Primary and KS3

North Wales  ·  Online Lessons

Hi, I'm Claire! I am a highly experienced and reliable former primary school teacher with 21 years in primary education, including 18 years at Christ Church, Moreton. I have extensive curriculum knowledge, strong adaptive teaching skills, and have held several leadership roles including English Lead, PE Lead, KS2 Lead, and member of the Senior Leadership Team. I have taught across the full primary phase, although I feel most confident and experienced in Key Stage 2. After leaving full-time teaching in July 2025 to pursue my passion for fitness, I have continued to stay involved in education through tutoring, which remains something I care deeply about.

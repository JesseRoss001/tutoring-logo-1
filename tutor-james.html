About James

Humanities (History, Geography, Religious Study) tutor for GCSE, and A-Level

Caerphilly  ·  Online Lessons

Hi, I am James. I am one of the History tutors here at North Wales Tutoring. I also teach Geography & Religious Studies. I currently have three years of tutoring experience covering both GCSE & A-Level on the WJEC, AQA & Edexcel exam boards.

As a tutor I make sure I get to know my students' strengths and weaknesses, what they enjoy and dislike, their learning styles and work around them while building on their weaknesses. I fond a casual approach to tutoring works the best, focusing on building repport and trust to ensure they feel they are able to tell me what they struggle with and what you need more help with.

ABOUT ME

Hello! I’m Jesse

I’m a qualified Secondary Mathematics teacher with a Bachelor’s in Chemical Engineering (Honours) from The University of Manchester and a Secondary Mathematics teaching qualification from Bangor University. As an experienced online tutor UK, I specialize in math and physics tutoring as well as chemistry A-Level tutoring and GCSE tutoring, all delivered 100% online.

Over the past several years, I’ve guided students through GCSE and A-Level curricula (WJEC, AQA, Edexcel, OCR, Eduqas, CAIE) in mathematics, physics, chemistry, and English. Whether you need a physics and maths tutor physics session or help with GCSE online courses, I’m here to ensure you achieve top grades.

My tutoring approach emphasizes individualized assessment, exam-focused strategies, and interactive lessons. Together, we’ll break down complex concepts into manageable steps, practice past papers, and build the confidence you need to succeed.

Teaching Approach

My tutoring philosophy combines personalised support, structured lesson plans, and a focus on exam success. I guide students through GCSE and A-Level with tailored strategies designed to boost understanding and confidence.

  • Individualised Assessments: We begin with a diagnostic test to pinpoint gaps, then tailor each lesson to your needs.
  • Exam-Focused Practice: Regular past-paper questions, timing strategies, and mark-scheme insights.
  • Interactive Learning: Virtual whiteboards, quizzes, and real-time feedback keep sessions engaging.
  • Ongoing Progress Tracking: Clear objectives and continuous feedback to ensure steady improvement.
